## Solution
The A1Z26 cipher is used for this challenge.  
A1Z26 is very simple direct substitution cipher, where each letter is replaced by its number in the alphabet (a -> 1, b -> 2, â€¦ , z -> 26).  
You can switch it back to the corresponding letters to obtain the flag.
